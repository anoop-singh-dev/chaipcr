SalivIQ™ Analyzer Optical Calibration

The optical calibration procedure corrects well-to-well differences in the SalivIQ™ Analyzer’s optical path. The calibration should be performed whenever the SalivIQ™ Analyzer is transported.

DURATION
~5 minutes
MATERIALS REQUIRED
SalivIQ™ Calibration Kit
*It is important to pipette the calibration solution from the same stock tube. If the solution is pipetted from different stock tubes, the stock solution should first be mixed.
